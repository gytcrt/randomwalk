# Random Walk (https://andreagao.com/)

> Personal blog of Andrea Gao, a startup founder building AI-native solutions in NYC. Covers GenAI applications, AI/ML systems, tech work culture, and personal essays.

## About author

Andrea Gao (she/her) is a startup founder based in NYC.

She spent most of her career helping organizations turn data into better decisions. As her work has increasingly focused on applying LLMs and GenAI to transform businesses' daily operations, she believes AI is more than hype. Her current venture builds AI-native solutions that work outside of demos and benefit both people and businesses.

She loves writing, which serves as a medium to present intriguing problems or shed light on unspeakable feelings. In her spare time, she is a big movie fan — a regular at the New York Film Festival and independent movie theaters.

This blog covers topics including:
- Artificial Intelligence and GenAI applications
- AI/ML system development and deployment
- Responsible AI and data bias
- Tech industry work culture and startup insights
- Writing and creative pursuits
- Personal essays and reflections
